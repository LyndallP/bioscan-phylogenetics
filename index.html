🔬 UK BIOSCAN Phylogenetics

Phylogenetic trees for UK BIOSCAN insect specimens placed onto BGE reference trees. Each family links to an interactive Taxonium viewer showing specimen placements, BAGS quality grades, and metadata.

How to use:
  1. Click a family name to open its tree in Taxonium (opens in a new tab).
  2. In Taxonium, use Color by to explore BAGS grades, placement quality, geography, and more.
  3. Click any tip node to see specimen metadata, BOLD links, and NCBI BLAST.
  4. Copy the browser URL after loading — it encodes the full view and is shareable without needing access to any files.
